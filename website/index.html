Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I integrate myclients with my website or Facebook?

2. Can I sync my agenda with Google calendar?

Yes. We offer 2-way synchronization with Google Calendar. You can export bookings from myclients to Google Calendar and also import events from Google Calendar to myclients to block those times.

3. Is there a limit for services?

No, you can create as many services, locations and service providers as you want.
